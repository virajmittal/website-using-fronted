About Us

Incorporated in 1990, as a non-profit organization, IMS society proudly stands today as the apex body of looking after three well known educational institutions in the city of Ghaziabad. IMS society was founded by a group of dynamic and visionary edupreneurs, with the noble idea of imparting world Since its inception, the IMS Group has promoted education in the various areas of Management Sciences, Information Technology, Bio Sciences, Engineering and Journalism and is continuously striving to achieve excellence in all its endeavours.
